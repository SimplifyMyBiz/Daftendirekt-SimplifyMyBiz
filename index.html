What we do

Three simple technologies

That help, not hinder, your business.

A beautiful website

Your storefront for the Internet, your website needs to convey your sales pitch in a simple and attractive format. Blog? We can help with that too. We use pre-designed templates that are easy and appealing to your customers. You won't be bombarded with questions about layouts and pixels, just point to the template you like and we do the rest.

Google Apps for Work

Instant Messages, Email, Documents, Spreadsheets, Voicemail--we set them up as a collaborative suite of tools to run the foundation of your business that are easily accessible from your computer, tablet or mobile device. All of these Google Tools are then hosted on your own domain, you@yourbusiness.com, giving a more professional look to your customer communications.

Apple Hardware

We help you choose the right Apple hardware and we set it up to be secure, backed up, connected to your iPhone and simple to use. Let Apple manage your contacts and your calendar so you can manage your business.

How it works

Three easy meetings

Meet with us three times and it's ready!

1st Meeting

We learn what you want for your business on the Internet. We find out what problems we can help solve using simple technology. If you like what you hear we shake hands and start! By the end of the 1st meeting we will have a website template picked out and a rough outline of the content.

2nd Meeting

The first draft of your website is previewed and we make a list of changes that are needed. A list of employees is created to then add to Google Apps and Apple iCloud.

3rd Meeting

The final product is launched at your business with a full day of side by side training and mentoring to get you productive.
Every Month- We include an hour of user support each month to continually help you with website updates, new employee training or any questions you may have. We want you to continue to grow your business without any headaches from your technology.

Useful Benefits

Simplify My Biz uses simple technology that just works.

A static HTML5 website

No servers that can crash or be hacked. The fastest way to deliver your content is with a static website.

Responsive Design

The site re-draws itself to match your customer's screen. Everything from large HD TV down to an iPhone will display beautifully and clear.

Amazon Cloud Hosting

Your website is hosted right alongside amazon.com using the same cloud technology that Amazon uses for themselves. Reliable, always up and secure.

Google Gmail

Use the same Gmail you know and love but with a professional looking you@yourbusiness.com address.

Google Docs

No need to spend hundreds on Microsoft Office licenses just to write a Word Doc, you have a simple and effective office suite built by Google and hosted in the cloud. Your Google Docs are on every computer you have and follow you on whatever device you need them on.
