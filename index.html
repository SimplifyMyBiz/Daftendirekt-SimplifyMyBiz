How it works

Three easy meetings

Meet with us three times and its finished and delivered along with training on how to use it.

1st Meeting

We learn what you want for your business on the internet. We find out what problems we can help solve using simple technology. If you like what you hear we shake hands and start! By the end of the 1st meeting we will have a website template picked out and a rough outline of the content.

2nd Meeting

The website first draft is previewed and we make a list of changes that are needed. A list of employees is created to then add to Google Apps and Apple iCloud.

3rd Meeting

The final product is launched at your business with a full day of side by side training and mentoring to get you productive.
Every Month- You have one hour of support for anything we built; website updates, training, new employee onboarding or any questions you might have. If it will be more than an hour we will quote you a time to get the job done. This leaves you with assurance that your business will continue to grow with all the computer headaches gone or at least managed by us.
